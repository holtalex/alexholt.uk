data collected

data is only collected when provided by you, this includes:

  • your name (when provided in the contact form)
  • your email address (when provided in the contact form)
  • a message to send to me (when provided in the contact form)

other data may automatically be sent to this site by your browser (e.g. your IP address), but this data is not stored by this site, though third-party services may store this data, more details on this can be found below.

third-parties

some third-party services are used by this site to allow it to function, but, where possible, the third-party services picked only collect the minimum data necessary.

these third-parties may transfer data outside of the uk, and laws may vary in these regions.

third-parties used

the following third-party services are used on this site:

  • cloudflare pages (for website hosting)
  • emailjs (for processing data submitted through the contact form)
  • rsms.me (for providing fonts)

each of these third-parties could collect technical data including your IP address, more details on this can be found in their respective privacy policies.

when you submit data via the contact form, this data is sent to emailjs so that they can email it to me. while emailjs does receive the data you provide in the form, it is not stored on their servers once the email to me has been sent.

my email provider is apple (icloud), and they will store the email containing the data you provide until i have deleted it, which will typically be when i respond to your message. more information about this can be found in apple's privacy policy.

links to third-party websites

this site my link to third-party websites, these are governed by their own privacy policies & terms of services, and i have no control over the data these sites collect.

when visiting any third-party websites, you should be cautious when providing any personal information, and it is recommended that you read the privacy policy & terms of service of any site you visit.

your rights

under UK law, you a variety of rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • your right of access
  • your right of rectification
  • your right to erasure
  • your right to restriction of processing
  • your right to object to processing
  • your right to data portability

if you wish to exercise any of your rights, please email privacy@alexholt.uk.

for requests to third-party services used by this site, please contact the third-party directly.

if you have concerns about how your personal data is handled, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).

details of how to lodge a complaint with the ICO can be found on their website.

contact

for any questions relating to this privacy policy, please email privacy@alexholt.uk.

for questions relating to third-party services, please contact the relevant third-party directly.
